About This Calculator

This drug dose calculator provides weight-based and age-based dosing for common medications used in India, following standard Indian and WHO guidelines.

How to Use

Enter the patient's weight in kg and age, select a drug category, and click Calculate. The tool will show the calculated dose, maximum dose, formulation volumes, and frequency.
