About This Calculator

This tool calculates weight-based drug doses for common medications used in India for both paediatric and adult patients. Enter the patient's weight, select the drug, and optionally enter your medicine's concentration to get the exact volume or tablet count.

How to Read Your Medicine Label

For syrups, the concentration is usually written as "Xmg/5ml" — divide X by 5 to get mg/ml. For example, Paracetamol 250mg/5ml = 50 mg/ml. For tablets, the strength is printed on the strip (e.g. "500mg").
